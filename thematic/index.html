In Between Falling and Rising

Independent bookstores are retail bookstores usually owned by individuals and operates on a medium to small scale. Since the bookstores are attached to local communities or embody beliefs held by their owners, the books they sell may include a narrower range of topics that touches certain issues. Without a doubt, independent bookstores hold a unique place both in the bookselling industry and in representing the cultures of various regions.

These critical members around the world, however, are now facing challenges concerning their survival due to difference in the supply chain among bookstores.
