In Between Falling and Rising

Independent bookstores are retail bookstores independently owned. Usually on a medium to small scale, the bookstores are more attached to local community or embody certain beliefs held by their owners. Independent bookstores without a doubt hold a unique place in not only the bookselling industry but the cultural prosperity of an area.

These minor yet critical members around the world, however, are now facing challenges concerning their survival due to, broadly speaking, different structures of supply chain among bookstores. In addition to urging the governments to compensate the inequality in the market, independent bookstores are taking actions as well to overcome the plight.
