Shedding Light On New Possibilities

Governments have enforced policies trying to improve the current condition such as implementing the Fixed-Book Price policy. As for independent bookstores, the owners have learned to form an association to make their voices better heard or to combine new strategy such as merging the original business with another one.

Fixed Book Price, also known as FBP, refers to a market mechanism that aims to regulate book price by laws or agreements. Mostly the targets of FBP are the ending retailer including markets, bookstores, or even convenience stores. By establishing Fixed Book Price among bookstores, the range of discounts would be regulated and even the selling price of books united.
